Burger

Delicious Burger.

Ingredients.

  • Burger buns
  • Ground beef (80/20)
  • Salt & pepper
  • Cheese (cheddar or American)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • >Onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayo
    •

    Steps to follow for the recipe.

    1. Form patties: 150–200 g each. Season both sides with salt & pepper.
    2. Preheat: Grill or skillet on medium-high. Lightly oil.
    3. Cook patties: 3–4 min per side (for medium). Don’t press them.
    4. Cheese: Add slice in the last 30–60 sec; cover to melt.
    5. Toast buns: 30–60 sec on the grill/pan with a little butter.
    6. Assemble: Bun → sauce → lettuce → patty → tomato → onion → pickles → more sauce → bun.
    7. Rest: 1–2 min.
    8. Serve: With fries or salad.
