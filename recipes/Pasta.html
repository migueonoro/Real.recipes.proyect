Pasta

Delicious Pasta.

Ingredients.

  • Pasta (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (2-3 cloves, minced)
  • Tomato sauce (1 cup)
  • Fresh basil (a few leaves, chopped)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Salt & pepper

Steps to follow for the recipe.

  1. Cook pasta: Boil in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté garlic: In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant (about 1-2 minutes).
  3. Add tomato sauce: Pour in the tomato sauce, stir, and let it simmer for 5-7 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Combine pasta and sauce: Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Garnish and serve: Sprinkle with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese before serving.
