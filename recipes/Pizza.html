Pizza

Delicious Pizza.

Ingredients.

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce (1/2 cup)
  • Mozzarella cheese (1-2 cups, shredded)
  • Pepperoni slices (optional)
  • Olive oil (1-2 tbsp)
  • Dried oregano (1 tsp)
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)

Steps to follow for the recipe.

  1. Preheat oven: Set to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.
  2. Prepare dough: Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Add sauce: Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Add cheese: Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add toppings: Place pepperoni slices or any other desired toppings on top of the cheese.
  6. Bake: Transfer pizza to the oven (on a baking sheet or pizza stone) and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  7. Garnish and serve: Remove pizza from the oven, drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with dried oregano, and garnish with fresh basil leaves. Slice and serve hot.
